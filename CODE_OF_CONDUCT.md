# Code of Conduct

humanize-flow contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, constructive, and focused on the work.

Please:

- Assume good intent.
- Be specific when reporting bugs or design concerns.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory language.
- Respect maintainers' time by reading documentation before asking for support.

Maintainers may remove disruptive content or block participation from contributors who repeatedly violate these expectations.
